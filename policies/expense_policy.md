# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Overview

Acme Corporation reimburses employees for reasonable business expenses incurred while performing their job duties. This policy outlines what is covered, spending limits, and the reimbursement process.

## General Principles

All expenses must be:
- **Necessary**: Required for business purposes
- **Reasonable**: Appropriate for the situation
- **Documented**: Supported by itemized receipts
- **Pre-approved**: When exceeding certain thresholds

## Expense Categories and Limits

### Travel Expenses

#### Airfare
- Book economy class for flights under 6 hours
- Business class permitted for flights over 6 hours with manager approval
- Book at least 14 days in advance when possible
- Use preferred booking tool: TravelPro

#### Hotels
- Maximum nightly rate by city tier:
  - **Tier 1** (NYC, SF, LA): $250/night
  - **Tier 2** (Chicago, Boston, Seattle): $200/night
  - **Tier 3** (All other cities): $150/night
- Book through TravelPro for best rates
- Loyalty program points belong to the employee

#### Ground Transportation
- Rental cars: Economy or mid-size class, maximum $75/day
- Ride-share (Uber/Lyft): Permitted for business travel, economy tier preferred
- Personal vehicle mileage: Reimbursed at $0.67/mile
- Parking and tolls: Reimbursed with receipts

### Meals

#### Per Diem Rates
When traveling for business:
- **Breakfast**: $20
- **Lunch**: $25
- **Dinner**: $45
- **Daily maximum**: $90

#### Client Entertainment
- Client meals: Up to $100/person with advance approval
- Team dinners: Up to $75/person with manager approval
- Alcohol: Maximum 2 drinks per person may be expensed

### Office Supplies
- Supplies under $50: No pre-approval required
- Supplies $50-$200: Manager approval required
- Supplies over $200: VP approval required

### Technology and Equipment
- Software subscriptions: Must use company procurement
- Hardware purchases: IT approval required
- Personal devices for work: See Remote Work Policy for stipends

### Professional Development
- Conference registration: Up to $2,000/year with manager approval
- Books and courses: Up to $500/year
- Professional memberships: Must be job-related, manager approval required

## Reimbursement Process

### Submitting Expenses

1. **Collect itemized receipts** for all expenses
2. **Submit within 30 days** of incurring the expense
3. **Use the expense portal**: expenses.acmecorp.com
4. **Include details**:
   - Date and location
   - Business purpose
   - Attendees (for meals/entertainment)
   - Project code if applicable

### Approval Workflow
- Expenses under $250: Manager approval
- Expenses $250-$1000: Director approval
- Expenses over $1000: VP approval
- Executive expenses: CFO approval

### Reimbursement Timeline
- Approved expenses are reimbursed within **10 business days**
- Reimbursements are processed via direct deposit
- Incomplete submissions will be returned for correction

## Corporate Credit Cards

Eligible employees may receive a corporate credit card:
- **Eligibility**: Manager level and above, or frequent travelers
- **Credit limit**: Based on role and travel frequency
- **Personal use prohibited**: Card is for business expenses only
- **Monthly reconciliation**: Required within 5 days of statement

## Non-Reimbursable Expenses

The following are NOT reimbursable:
- Personal entertainment (movies, gyms, streaming services)
- Personal travel or meals
- Traffic tickets or parking violations
- Airline club memberships (unless pre-approved)
- Spouse/partner travel expenses
- First-class upgrades (unless medically required)
- Excessive minibar charges
- Lost or stolen personal items

## International Travel

For international business travel:
- Obtain pre-approval from department VP
- Ensure valid passport (minimum 6 months validity)
- Register with company travel security program
- Currency conversion: Use exchange rate on expense date
- Retain all receipts in any currency

## Audit and Compliance

- Expenses are subject to random audits
- Fraudulent expense claims will result in termination
- Managers are responsible for verifying team expenses
- Finance reviews all expenses over $500

## Exceptions

Exceptions to this policy require:
- Written justification
- VP or higher approval
- Finance review
