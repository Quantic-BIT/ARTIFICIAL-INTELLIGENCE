# Employee Benefits Policy

## Overview

Acme Corporation offers a comprehensive benefits package to support employee health, financial security, and work-life balance. This document summarizes the benefits available to eligible employees.

## Eligibility

### Full-Time Employees
Full-time employees (40+ hours/week) are eligible for all benefits starting on the first of the month following 30 days of employment.

### Part-Time Employees
Part-time employees (20-39 hours/week) are eligible for:
- Healthcare benefits (prorated premium contribution)
- 401(k) participation
- Employee Assistance Program

### Waiting Period
Most benefits have a 30-day waiting period from hire date. 401(k) has no waiting period.

## Health Insurance

### Medical Plans

We offer three medical plan options through BlueCross BlueShield:

| Plan | Employee Monthly | Family Monthly | Deductible | Max Out-of-Pocket |
|------|-----------------|----------------|------------|-------------------|
| Basic PPO | $150 | $450 | $1,500 | $6,000 |
| Standard PPO | $225 | $575 | $750 | $4,000 |
| Premium PPO | $300 | $700 | $250 | $2,500 |

**Company Contribution**: Acme pays 75% of employee premiums and 50% of dependent premiums.

### Dental Insurance
- **Provider**: Delta Dental
- **Coverage**: 100% preventive, 80% basic, 50% major
- **Annual maximum**: $2,000 per person
- **Employee cost**: $25/month (employee), $60/month (family)

### Vision Insurance
- **Provider**: VSP
- **Coverage**: Annual eye exam, frames/contacts allowance
- **Frame allowance**: $200 every 24 months
- **Contact lens allowance**: $150 annually
- **Employee cost**: $10/month (employee), $25/month (family)

## Retirement Benefits

### 401(k) Plan

- **Eligibility**: All employees, no waiting period
- **Employee contribution**: Up to IRS limit ($23,000 in 2025)
- **Company match**: 100% match on first 4% of salary
- **Vesting**: Immediate vesting of employee contributions
- **Company match vesting**: 25% per year, fully vested after 4 years
- **Investment options**: 20+ mutual funds, target-date funds

### Retirement Planning
- Free access to financial advisors
- Quarterly retirement planning webinars
- Annual 401(k) checkup meetings

## Life and Disability Insurance

### Life Insurance
- **Basic Life**: 1x annual salary (company paid)
- **Supplemental Life**: Up to 5x salary (employee paid)
- **Spouse coverage**: Up to $50,000 available
- **Child coverage**: Up to $10,000 available

### Disability Insurance

**Short-Term Disability**:
- 60% of salary for up to 26 weeks
- 14-day waiting period
- Company paid

**Long-Term Disability**:
- 60% of salary after short-term ends
- Up to age 65 or Social Security eligibility
- Company paid

## Flexible Spending Accounts

### Healthcare FSA
- Pre-tax contributions up to $3,050/year
- Use for medical, dental, vision expenses
- Use-it-or-lose-it (with $610 rollover)

### Dependent Care FSA
- Pre-tax contributions up to $5,000/year
- Use for childcare, elder care
- Must be used within plan year

### Commuter Benefits
- Pre-tax transit/parking benefits
- Up to $315/month for transit
- Up to $315/month for parking

## Paid Leave

### Parental Leave
- **Birth parents**: 16 weeks paid at 100%
- **Non-birth parents**: 8 weeks paid at 100%
- **Adoption**: 12 weeks paid at 100%
- Must be taken within 12 months of birth/adoption

### Bereavement Leave
- **Immediate family**: 5 days paid
- **Extended family**: 3 days paid
- **Close friend**: 1 day paid

### Jury Duty
- Full pay for duration of service
- Present jury summons to HR
- Return to work when not needed

### Voting Leave
- Up to 2 hours paid for voting
- Coordinate with manager for timing

## Professional Development

### Tuition Reimbursement
- Up to $5,250/year for degree programs
- Must be job-related
- Grade requirement: B or better
- Requires 1-year commitment after completion

### Conference and Training
- Budget: Up to $2,000/year per employee
- Manager approval required
- Submit through expense system

### LinkedIn Learning
- Free access for all employees
- Unlimited courses
- Certificate programs available

## Wellness Benefits

### Employee Assistance Program (EAP)
- Free confidential counseling (up to 8 sessions)
- Legal and financial consultation
- Work-life balance resources
- 24/7 hotline: 1-800-EAP-HELP

### Wellness Stipend
- $600/year wellness reimbursement
- Use for gym memberships, fitness equipment, mental health apps
- Submit receipts through expense system

### Wellness Programs
- Annual biometric screenings
- Flu shots (on-site)
- Mental health workshops
- Ergonomic assessments

## Enrollment

### Open Enrollment
- Annual enrollment period: November 1-15
- Changes effective January 1
- No changes outside enrollment unless qualifying life event

### Qualifying Life Events
Changes allowed within 30 days of:
- Marriage/divorce
- Birth/adoption
- Loss of other coverage
- Change in dependent status

- Benefits portal: benefits.acmecorp.com

## COBRA

Upon termination, you may continue health coverage through COBRA:
- Coverage up to 18 months (36 for some events)
- You pay full premium plus 2% admin fee
- Enrollment information provided at termination
