# Acme Corporation Employee Handbook

## Welcome to Acme Corporation

Welcome to Acme Corporation! We are excited to have you join our team. This handbook outlines our company policies and procedures to help you succeed in your role.

## About Acme Corporation

**Mission Statement:** To deliver innovative solutions that empower our customers and create lasting value for our stakeholders.

**Core Values:**
- **Integrity**: We act with honesty and transparency in all our dealings
- **Innovation**: We continuously seek better ways to solve problems
- **Collaboration**: We work together to achieve common goals
- **Excellence**: We strive for the highest quality in everything we do
- **Respect**: We treat everyone with dignity and fairness

## Organizational Structure

Acme Corporation is organized into the following departments:
- **Executive Leadership**: CEO, CFO, CTO, COO
- **Human Resources**: Employee relations, benefits, recruiting
- **Engineering**: Product development, IT infrastructure
- **Sales & Marketing**: Business development, customer acquisition
- **Operations**: Day-to-day business operations
- **Finance**: Accounting, budgeting, financial planning

## Employment Classification

### Full-Time Employees
Employees who work 40 hours or more per week are classified as full-time and are eligible for all company benefits.

### Part-Time Employees
Employees who work less than 30 hours per week are classified as part-time. Part-time employees receive prorated benefits based on hours worked.

### Contract Workers
Independent contractors are not employees of Acme Corporation and are not eligible for employee benefits.

## Work Hours

**Standard Business Hours:** Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM local time.

**Core Hours:** All employees are expected to be available during core hours of 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM for meetings and collaboration.

**Flexible Scheduling:** With manager approval, employees may adjust their start and end times as long as they:
- Complete their required hours
- Are available during core hours
- Meet all deadlines and commitments

## Probationary Period

All new employees undergo a 90-day probationary period. During this time:
- Performance will be closely monitored
- Regular feedback sessions will be conducted
- Employment may be terminated at will by either party

## Performance Reviews

Performance reviews are conducted:
- **Quarterly**: Brief check-ins with direct managers
- **Annually**: Comprehensive reviews that impact compensation and promotions

Performance is evaluated based on:
1. Achievement of goals and objectives
2. Quality of work
3. Teamwork and collaboration
4. Professional development
5. Adherence to company values

## Termination of Employment

### Voluntary Resignation
Employees wishing to resign should provide:
- **Non-management**: 2 weeks written notice
- **Management**: 4 weeks written notice

### Involuntary Termination
The company reserves the right to terminate employment for:
- Policy violations
- Poor performance
- Misconduct
- Business restructuring
