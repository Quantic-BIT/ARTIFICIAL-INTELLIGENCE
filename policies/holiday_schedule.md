# Company Holiday Schedule 2025

## Overview

Acme Corporation observes the following holidays in 2025. All offices are closed on these days, and full-time employees receive paid time off.

## Official Company Holidays

| Holiday | Date | Day of Week |
|---------|------|-------------|
| New Year's Day | January 1, 2025 | Wednesday |
| Martin Luther King Jr. Day | January 20, 2025 | Monday |
| Presidents' Day | February 17, 2025 | Monday |
| Memorial Day | May 26, 2025 | Monday |
| Juneteenth | June 19, 2025 | Thursday |
| Independence Day | July 4, 2025 | Friday |
| Labor Day | September 1, 2025 | Monday |
| Thanksgiving Day | November 27, 2025 | Thursday |
| Day After Thanksgiving | November 28, 2025 | Friday |
| Christmas Eve | December 24, 2025 | Wednesday |
| Christmas Day | December 25, 2025 | Thursday |

**Total: 12 paid holidays per year**

## Floating Holidays

In addition to official holidays, employees receive **2 floating holidays** per year:
- Use for religious observances, personal celebrations, or any purpose
- Must be scheduled with manager approval
- Do not carry over to the next year
- Not paid out upon termination

## Holiday Pay

### Full-Time Employees
- Receive regular pay for holidays
- Must work scheduled days before and after holiday to receive pay
- Exceptions for approved PTO

### Part-Time Employees
- Holiday pay prorated based on regular schedule
- Example: 20-hour/week employee receives 4 hours holiday pay

### Holiday Work
If required to work on a holiday:
- Receive 1.5x regular pay for hours worked
- Plus an alternative day off (schedule with manager)
- Advance notice will be provided when possible

## Holiday Closures

### Standard Office Hours
Offices are completely closed on holidays listed above.

### Reduced Schedule Periods
Between Christmas and New Year's:
- December 26-30: Offices open with reduced staff
- Essential operations only
- Many employees use PTO during this period

### Emergency Support
For business-critical issues during holidays:
- IT Emergency Line: 1-800-ACME-911
- Security: Contact building security
- On-call schedules managed by department

## Religious and Cultural Observances

Acme Corporation respects diverse religious and cultural traditions:
- Use floating holidays for religious observances
- Request unpaid leave for additional observances
- Discuss accommodations with your manager
- We do not observe all religious holidays as company holidays

## Holiday Decorations

Guidelines for holiday decorations in the workplace:
- Keep decorations professional and inclusive
- Remove by January 5th
- No decorations that could be considered offensive
- Common areas decorated by Facilities team

## International Offices

Employees in international offices observe:
- Local public holidays for their country
- Some global company holidays (as determined by regional management)
- Contact your local HR for specific holiday schedule

## Scheduling Considerations

### Meetings
- Avoid scheduling meetings on holidays
- Be mindful of holidays when working with international colleagues
- Mark holidays on your calendar

### Deadlines
- Plan deliverables around holiday schedules
- Communicate early if holidays affect timelines
- Consider reduced staffing during holiday weeks

### Coverage
- Departments must plan for holiday coverage
- Discuss coverage needs in advance
- Rotate holiday work fairly
