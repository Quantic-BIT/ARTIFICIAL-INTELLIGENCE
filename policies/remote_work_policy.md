# Remote Work Policy

## Overview

Acme Corporation supports flexible work arrangements including remote work. This policy outlines guidelines for employees who work from home or other remote locations.

## Eligibility

Remote work eligibility is determined by:
- **Role suitability**: Not all positions can be performed remotely
- **Performance history**: Employees must be in good standing
- **Manager approval**: Direct manager must approve remote work arrangements
- **Tenure**: Minimum 90 days of employment (probation completion)

## Remote Work Options

### Fully Remote
- Work from home 100% of the time
- May be required to visit office quarterly for team meetings
- Must be within reasonable travel distance for occasional in-person events

### Hybrid Remote
- Work from home 2-3 days per week
- Required in-office days will be determined by department
- Standard hybrid schedule: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday in office

### Occasional Remote
- Work from home on an as-needed basis
- Requires advance notice to manager
- Maximum 4 remote days per month without formal arrangement

## Remote Work Requirements

### Workspace
Employees must maintain a dedicated workspace that is:
- Quiet and free from distractions
- Ergonomically appropriate
- Secure for handling company information
- Has reliable internet connection (minimum 25 Mbps download)

### Equipment
The company provides:
- Laptop computer
- Required software licenses
- VPN access
- Video conferencing tools

Employees are responsible for:
- High-speed internet connection
- Desk and chair (one-time $500 stipend available)
- Phone for business calls (company phone or reimbursement available)

### Communication Expectations
Remote employees must:
- Be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM local time)
- Respond to messages within 2 hours during work hours
- Keep calendar updated with availability
- Use video for team meetings
- Maintain regular communication with team and manager

### Security Requirements
When working remotely:
- Use company VPN for accessing internal systems
- Lock computer when stepping away
- Do not use public WiFi for sensitive work
- Store physical documents securely
- Report any security concerns immediately

## Performance Expectations

Remote employees are held to the same performance standards as in-office employees:
- Meet all deadlines and deliverables
- Maintain productivity levels
- Participate actively in team meetings
- Complete required training
- Be responsive to colleagues

## Remote Work Stipends

### Monthly Internet Stipend
- $50/month for remote and hybrid employees
- Requires submission of internet bill

### Home Office Setup Stipend
- One-time $500 stipend for new remote arrangements
- For ergonomic equipment (desk, chair, monitor)
- Requires receipts and manager approval

## Time Tracking

Remote employees must:
- Log work hours in the time tracking system
- Take required breaks (see Work Hours policy)
- Report overtime in advance
- Maintain accurate records

## Meetings and Collaboration

### Required In-Person Events
- Quarterly all-hands meetings
- Annual company retreat
- Department planning sessions
- Training sessions (as designated)

### Virtual Meeting Etiquette
- Camera on for team meetings
- Mute when not speaking
- Use professional virtual background if needed
- Be punctual and prepared

## Revocation of Remote Work

Remote work privileges may be revoked if:
- Performance declines
- Communication issues arise
- Security policies are violated
- Business needs require on-site presence
- Abuse of the policy is detected

## International Remote Work

Working from outside the country requires:
- Prior HR and legal approval
- Maximum 4 weeks per year
- Compliance with local tax and employment laws
- Appropriate visa status

## Request Process

To request a remote work arrangement:
1. Discuss with your direct manager
2. Submit formal request through HR portal
3. Complete remote work agreement form
4. Set up security and equipment
5. Begin approved arrangement
